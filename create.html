Tools of the trade

Some of the tools we use to get big products and services to market quickly.

Co-Design

No-one understands your business better than you, we have lots of structured techniques that get your teams hands dirty in the process of strategy and design. It gets better results and upskills your team with powerful design thinking approaches and frameworks.

Discovery Workshops

When teams are big and time is short we have a structured “Audit, Dreamer, Realist” workshop process that quickly extracts the knowledge necessary to create momentum and empower teams to work autonomously.

Co-Location

No Ta-Da moments, working in the same space as your team (your place or ours) helps us make decisions quicker keeping the process visible to the wider team.

Sprints

We break down huge problems into sprints with frequent checkins so your organisation has a clear view on what the team is working on, but the team also has the flexibility to drive innovation through multiple experiments, options and approaches.

Prototyping

With large teams and complex problems comes confusion. Paper, static, Live data, and service scenario prototyping allows us to simultaneously validate technical and user needs, as well as engage ‘hard to get hold of managers’ with dynamic presentations.

User Centered Design

We dont go more than 20 hours without testing with real users. Even during the research and strategic process we are putting prototypes and hands on experiments in front of users to truly understand what they need and how to deliver value.
